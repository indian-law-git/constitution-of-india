# Part VII — The States in Part B of the First Schedule

*[Part VII repealed by section 29 + Schedule of The Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956. The Part B State category — formerly comprising Hyderabad, Jammu and Kashmir, Madhya Bharat, Mysore, PEPSU, Rajasthan, Saurashtra, Travancore-Cochin and Vindhya Pradesh — was eliminated by the same Act, with most of these former Part B States merging into reorganised Part A States or becoming Union territories. Article 238, which was the sole article of this Part, was repealed too.]*
