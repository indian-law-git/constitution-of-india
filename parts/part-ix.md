# Part IX — The territories in Part D of the First Schedule and other territories not specified in that Schedule

*[Part IX repealed by section 29 + Schedule of The Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956. The Part D territory category was eliminated by the same Act; what were Part D territories (the Andaman and Nicobar Islands) became Union territories under the new Part VIII regime. Article 243, which was the sole article of this Part, was repealed too — Article 240 now governs Union territories.]*
