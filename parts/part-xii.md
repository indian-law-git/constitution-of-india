# Part XII — Finance, Property, Contracts and Suits

This Part contains Articles 264 through 300, with article 300A inserted by 44A as the sole article of the new Chapter IV. See individual article files in `articles/`.

Chapters in this Part:

- Chapter I — Finance (articles 264–291)
- Chapter II — Borrowing (articles 292–293)
- Chapter III — Property, Contracts, Rights, Liabilities, Obligations and Suits (articles 294–300)
- Chapter IV — Right to Property (article 300A; inserted by 44A §34)
