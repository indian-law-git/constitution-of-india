# Tenth Schedule

*[Section 5(d) of the 36th Amendment omitted the Tenth Schedule. The associated-state framework for Sikkim was replaced by full-State status: Sikkim was inserted into the First Schedule as State entry 22, allotted Schedule 4 seat 22, and given a new Article 371F with the special provisions for the new State.]*

*Note: A new Tenth Schedule on disqualification of members on ground of defection was added by the Constitution (Fifty-second Amendment) Act, 1985. The "Sikkim" Tenth Schedule documented here lived only between 35A (22 February 1975) and 36A (16 May 1975) — approximately twelve weeks.*
