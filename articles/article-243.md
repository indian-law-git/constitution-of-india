# Article 243. Administration of territories specified in Part D of the First Schedule and other territories not specified in that Schedule

*[Section 29 + Schedule of the 7th Amendment repealed Part IX (the part containing Article 243 — about Part D territories administration). Part D territory category was eliminated by the same Act; Article 240 now governs Union territories.]*
