# Article 306. Power of certain States in Part B of the First Schedule to impose restrictions on trade and commerce

*[Section 29 + Schedule of the 7th Amendment expressly omitted Article 306 (power of certain States to impose restrictions on trade and commerce). The provision was about Part B States and is no longer needed.]*
