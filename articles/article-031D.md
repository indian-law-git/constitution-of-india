# Article 31D. Saving of laws in respect of anti-national activities

*[Article 31D omitted by section 2 of The Constitution (Forty-third Amendment) Act, 1977. The 42A-inserted provision had given Parliament exclusive power to legislate against "anti-national activities" and "anti-national associations" and immunised such laws from Part III rights review. The Statement of Objects and Reasons described these powers as "of a sweeping nature" and "capable of abuse".]*
