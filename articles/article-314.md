# Article 314. Provision for protection of existing officers of certain services

*[Section 3 of the 28th Amendment omitted Article 314. The protection of pre-Constitution Secretary-of-State appointees' service conditions is now subject to Parliament's variation/revocation power under new Article 312A inserted by the same Act.]*
