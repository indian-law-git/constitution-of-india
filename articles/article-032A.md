# Article 32A. Constitutional validity of State laws not to be considered in proceedings under article 32

Notwithstanding anything in article 32, the Supreme Court shall not consider the constitutional validity of any State law in any proceedings under that article unless the constitutional validity of any Central law is also in issue in such proceedings.
