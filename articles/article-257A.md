# Article 257A. Assistance to States by deployment of armed forces or other forces of the Union

*[Article 257A omitted by section 33 of The Constitution (Forty-fourth Amendment) Act, 1978. The 42A-inserted provision had empowered the Union to deploy armed forces in any State for "grave situation of law and order", with such forces acting under Union directions and outside State control.]*
