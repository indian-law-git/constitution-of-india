# Article 329A. Special provision as to elections to Parliament in the case of Prime Minister and Speaker

*[Article 329A omitted by section 36 of The Constitution (Forty-fourth Amendment) Act, 1978. The 39A-inserted provision had immunised the elections of the Prime Minister and Speaker from court challenge and had retroactively validated such elections. Clause (4) was struck down in Indira Nehru Gandhi v. Raj Narain (1975) as violating the basic structure (free and fair elections). 44A omits the entire article.]*
