# Article 144A. Special provisions as to disposal of questions relating to constitutional validity of laws

*[Article 144A omitted by section 5(1) of The Constitution (Forty-third Amendment) Act, 1977. A saving in section 5(2) allowed the Supreme Court to dispose of pending cases as if 144A had been omitted from 1 February 1977. The Supreme Court had earlier expressed in Misrilal Jain v. State of Orissa (AIR 1977 SC 1686) that the mandated minimum bench of seven and the two-thirds majority requirement should be reconsidered so that the Court could decide bench size case-by-case.]*
