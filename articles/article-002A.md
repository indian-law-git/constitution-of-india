# Article 2A. Sikkim to be associated with the Union

*[Section 5(a) of the 36th Amendment omitted Article 2A. The associated-state framework introduced by 35A was replaced by full-State status for Sikkim — Sikkim was inserted into the First Schedule as State entry 22 and a new Article 371F was added by 36A with the special provisions for the new State.]*
