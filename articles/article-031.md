# Article 31. Compulsory acquisition of property

*[Article 31 omitted by section 6 of The Constitution (Forty-fourth Amendment) Act, 1978. The right to property ceased to be a fundamental right; it was recreated as a legal right in the new article 300A under the new Chapter IV of Part XII (inserted by section 34 of the same Act): "No person shall be deprived of his property save by authority of law." The compensation safeguard for educational institutions of minorities (formerly the proviso to article 31(2)) was preserved as the new clause (1A) of article 30. The "Right to Property" sub-heading after article 30 was also omitted by section 5 of this Act.]*
